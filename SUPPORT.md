## Report a bug

If something is broken:

1. check whether it is reproducible on the latest `main`
2. gather logs, screenshots, or failing steps
3. open an issue with enough detail to reproduce it

If the bug is security-sensitive, do **not** open a public issue. Use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Feature ideas

For feature ideas, the most useful reports include:

- the problem you are trying to solve
- why MapMind is the right place for it
- what the user-facing change would look like
- what should explicitly stay out of scope
